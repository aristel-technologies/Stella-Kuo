AI-RAN

By Stella Kuo 郭亞璇

𝐀𝐈-𝐑𝐀𝐍 𝐢𝐬𝐧'𝐭 𝐚𝐛𝐨𝐮𝐭 𝐚𝐝𝐝𝐢𝐧𝐠 𝐀𝐈 𝐭𝐨 𝐭𝐞𝐥𝐞𝐜𝐨𝐦 𝐧𝐞𝐭𝐰𝐨𝐫𝐤𝐬.

It's about transforming telecom networks into AI platforms.

For years, mobile network operators have faced the same fundamental challenge:

· Mobile traffic continues to grow.

· Revenue growth remains relatively flat.

At the same time, a significant portion of RAN compute resources remains underutilized outside peak traffic periods.

𝐀𝐈-𝐑𝐀𝐍 𝐜𝐡𝐚𝐧𝐠𝐞𝐬 𝐭𝐡𝐚𝐭 𝐞𝐪𝐮𝐚𝐭𝐢𝐨𝐧.

By enabling RAN and AI workloads to run on the same accelerated infrastructure, operators can:

· Increase infrastructure utilization

· Reduce the total cost of ownership (TCO) through resource consolidation

· Unlock new revenue opportunities by hosting Edge AI services

The real transformation isn't just technological. It's economic.

For decades, telecom operators have built their business around monetizing connectivity.

AI-RAN introduces the opportunity to monetize compute.

As AI inference moves closer to the network edge, infrastructure efficiency will become just as important as radio performance.
