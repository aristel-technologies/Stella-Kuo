About Me

I am Stella Kuo (йғӯдәһз’Ү), also known as Goro Arielle Stella, an AI Infrastructure and Telecom Systems Engineer focused on the convergence of telecommunications, artificial intelligence, and high-performance computing.


My career journey started in network engineering, where I developed a strong foundation in switching, routing, and communication systems. I later moved into 4G/5G networks, Open RAN, and telecom system integration, working on technologies that enable the next generation of connected systems.


Today, I focus on AI infrastructure, GPU accelerated computing, and enterprise AI platforms. My goal is to help build intelligent networks where connectivity, computing, and artificial intelligence work together to create scalable solutions for the future.
